🔒 Privacy Policy

📅 Last Revised: March 25, 2026 – Rooted in Transparency

Your privacy matters deeply to the Phyterra Verdant Codex. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website. Our commitment reflects the same integrity we apply to soil stewardship.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data: We may collect your name, email address, or other contact details only when voluntarily submitted via newsletter signups, contact forms, or event registrations. You are never required to provide personal data to read the Codex.
b. Non-Personal Data: Like most websites, we automatically collect usage data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent). This helps us improve content performance and understand how gardeners engage with our articles.
c. Cookies & Tracking: We use essential and analytical cookies to enhance user experience. For detailed information, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data to: (i) deliver the Verdant Codex content and optimize site functionality; (ii) respond to inquiries or gardening questions; (iii) send occasional newsletters if you opt-in (you can unsubscribe anytime); (iv) analyze aggregate trends to create better educational resources; (v) prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, firewalls) to protect against unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined or as required by law. If you wish to request deletion of your data, please email privacy@phyterraverdant.org.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you reside in the European Economic Area or California, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to restrict processing. To exercise these rights, contact us with the subject “Data Request”. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Links & Services

Our pages may reference external gardening tools or seed banks. Those sites have separate privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. Phyterra is not responsible for third-party content or privacy practices.
