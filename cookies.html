    🌾 What Are Cookies? (A Gardener’s Analogy)

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Think of them as name tags for your visit — they help the site remember your preferences, language settings, or whether you’ve agreed to policies. Like a garden marker that reminds you where you planted tomatoes, cookies serve basic memory functions. We only plant the essential kind — no invasive species.

    🔐 How We Protect Your Data (Soil-to-Screen Ethics)

    Our website uses HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS) ensuring all data exchanged between your browser and our servers remains private. We do not combine cookie data with any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it through a contact form or newsletter signup (and even then, your data is stored securely and never shared). Any personal data you entrust to us (like name and email for correspondence) is treated as confidential correspondence — like a sealed letter. We retain it only as long as necessary to respond and fulfill your request.

    We undergo annual digital stewardship audits, and our hosting provider is selected based on green energy usage and data protection standards compliant with GDPR and ePrivacy Directive. Though our roots are in the soil, our digital infrastructure respects the most protective international privacy frameworks.

    🍪 Cookie FAQ — Digging Deeper

    Does Phyterra use cookies for advertising or retargeting?

    Never. We do not run ads, affiliate marketing, or retargeting campaigns. Our funding comes from community donations, seed sales, and workshops — not from selling user data or attention.

    What about embedded content like YouTube videos or Vimeo?

    We avoid embedding third-party media that sets cookies without consent. On rare occasions when we embed a workshop recording, we use privacy-enhanced modes (e.g., youtube-nocookie.com) and inform users. You can watch those without any tracking if you decline third-party cookies in your browser.

    Are there cookies in the downloadable garden planners?

    No. Our PDF guides, seed logs, and e-books are completely free of any scripts or tracking elements — pure garden knowledge.

    Can I visit the site without any cookies at all?

    Yes. Our core content (the Verdant Codex articles, plant databases, and community stories) is accessible even if you block all cookies via browser settings. You may lose session-based features like saving your favourite articles, but the knowledge remains open.
